Roasters





Our main coffee roaster is Caravan Coffee located in Newberg, OR. We have used Caravan Coffee since 2010 when Meredith called them in a panic not knowing how much coffee and which types she should order a couple weeks before Uncommon Grounds opened. Since then they have constantly provided incredible coffee, education, and friendship. Caravan coffee is all about their relationship with the coffee growers and the coffee consumers. Their coffee Hand-Crafted, responsibly sourced, and optimally roasted-to-order. Caravan Coffee ethically sources coffee from all over the world and we love them for that. In fact, we just really love them in general.

Check them out here

On Rotation

We believe there are so many amazing roasters with outstanding coffee all over and it makes us sad to think we are missing out on drinking delicious coffee. To solve this problem, we decided that each month we would have a different guest roaster. That means that, not only do you get the classically marvelous Caravan Coffee, you also get to try coffee from places all over the US. We call that a win win situation right there.

Here are some of the fantastic roaster we have partner with, we love them and we hope you do as well!

About

History

We have been a coffee shop since the dawn of time. Well not really since then, but for at least 12 years, and it has been under the current management for almost five years. These past five years we have been focusing on producing the most delicious coffee possible while also having the most fun possible.

Beliefs

We believe that joy should abound in all things, including drinking coffee. We are community and joy driven. While we believe that producing a wonderful cup of coffee is important we believe customers are even more important. At Uncommon Grounds we are all family. One giant messy family that loves coffee and each other.

Welcome to the family, we can't wait to have a cup of joe with you and get to know you better.
